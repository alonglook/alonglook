New Revised Edition of How To Cook Everything by Mark Bittman

September 26, 2019

The 20th anniversary of How to Cook Everything by Mark Bittman is marked by this new edition with photographs by me! It was an honor to work on such a classic and essential book. We spent 3 weeks in NYC photographing hundreds of dishes and my crew, Victoria Granof (food stylist extraordinaire), Philippa Brathwaite (prop stylist), Martyna Szczesna (photo assistance), Kerri Conan, Stephanie Fletcher from Houghton Mifflin Harcourt made the job a dream. Dinner for Everyone by Mark Bittman – out in stores now!

September 26, 2019

The book that really solves the nightly dinner dilemna is out now! Dinner for Everyone by Mark Bittman was a lengthy shoot with over 300 recipes and lots of leftovers (I gained a few pounds on this shoot!)

Newlywed Table cookbook – Available now!

April 17, 2018

Earlier this year we were busy shooting a cookbook with Maria Zizka for Artisan books titled The Newlywed Table. We shot mostly in studio but also on a couple locations (including my own kitchen :). Graham Bradley designed the book and cheerfully mocked up over 200 cover options – this team was a hard working, creative powerhouse!
